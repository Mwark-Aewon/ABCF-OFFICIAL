YAEL Ministry Small Group Registrations Open

For School Students | Deadline: April 10, 2026
YAEL Ministry – our school-based fellowship formed entirely by small groups – is now accepting new members! We support students in their faith journey through peer gatherings, mentorship, campus mission work, and life skill workshops. Small groups will be assigned based on school and grade level to ensure close-knit connections that help students grow as leaders and followers of Christ.

Pulse Ministry Sports Fest & Faith Camp

For Teens (13-18) | Date: April 20-22, 2026
Get ready for Pulse Ministry’s biggest event of the quarter! Our Sports Fest on April 20 will include basketball, volleyball, and fun games – all centered on biblical values of teamwork and respect. This will be followed by a 2-day Faith Camp focused on discipleship and community building. Snacks, accommodation, and materials are all provided.
